Phone Information Risks & Misuse

Data Theft

Personal information, contacts, and private messages can be stolen through malicious apps and phishing attacks.

67% of users unaware

Location Tracking

Apps can track your location without consent, creating detailed movement patterns and privacy violations.

89% of apps track location

Camera/Mic Access

Unauthorized access to camera and microphone can lead to blackmail, stalking, and privacy invasion.

45% grant unnecessary access

Financial Fraud

Banking apps and payment information can be compromised, leading to financial losses and identity theft.

$2.7B lost to mobile fraud
